What is a Flatbed?

A flatbed trailer is an open-deck trailer with no sides or roof, designed to transport oversized, heavy, or irregularly shaped cargo that doesn’t fit in standard trailers.

  • Open design for easy loading from all sides
  • Ideal for oversized and heavy equipment
  • Flexible for multiple industries

Why Choose a Flatbed?

Flatbeds provide unmatched versatility in hauling loads of all shapes and sizes.

  • Versatility: Perfect for machinery, construction materials, and oversized loads.
  • Accessibility: Freight can be loaded/unloaded with cranes or forklifts from any angle.
  • High Demand: Flatbeds are always in demand for specialized freight.
