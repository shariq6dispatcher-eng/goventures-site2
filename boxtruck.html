What is a Box Truck?

A box truck, also known as a straight truck or cube van, is a type of commercial vehicle designed with an enclosed, rectangular cargo area. Unlike semi-trailers, the cargo box is mounted directly onto the truck’s chassis, making it a single, solid unit.

  • Fully enclosed cargo protection
  • Great for retail, furniture & appliances
  • Easy maneuvering in cities

Why Choose a Box Truck?

Whether you’re a small business owner, independent contractor, or part of a logistics fleet, a box truck is a reliable solution for safe and efficient transportation.

  • Security: Fully enclosed cargo protects goods from theft and weather.
  • Versatility: Suitable for a wide range of industries and services.
  • Ease of Use: Smaller than tractor-trailers, making them easier to maneuver in cities.
