Power Only Dispatching

Reliable dispatching for carriers offering power only trucking solutions.

What is Power Only?

Power Only trucking means carriers provide just the tractor while shippers or brokers provide the trailer. It offers flexibility and reduces downtime for carriers.

  • No need to own trailers
  • Flexible freight opportunities
  • Consistent demand

Why Choose Power Only?

Power Only is great for maximizing uptime while reducing overhead costs tied to trailer ownership.

  • Lower equipment investment
  • Access to diverse freight
  • Keep moving without trailer hassles
