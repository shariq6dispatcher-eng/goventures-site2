What is a Reefer?

A reefer (refrigerated truck) is used to transport perishable and temperature-sensitive goods such as food, beverages, and pharmaceuticals under controlled conditions.

  • Maintains specific temperatures for goods
  • Essential for food and pharma industries
  • Year-round high demand

Why Choose a Reefer?

Reefers provide steady freight opportunities and higher rates due to specialized requirements.

  • Access to temperature-sensitive freight
  • Strong earning potential
  • Consistent loads year-round
