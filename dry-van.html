What is a Dry Van?

A dry van is the most common type of freight trailer used in trucking and logistics. It is a fully enclosed, non-climate-controlled trailer designed to transport general cargo.

  • Fully enclosed for maximum protection
  • Ideal for non-perishable goods
  • Standard in logistics and supply chain

Why Choose a Dry Van?

Dry vans are the backbone of freight transportation across the country..

  • Versatility: Can haul a wide range of dry, packaged, and boxed goods.
  • Security: Enclosed trailer keeps cargo safe from theft and weather.
  • Efficiency: Standardized equipment makes it easy to load, unload, and transport across all regions.
